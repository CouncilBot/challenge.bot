How It Works

Once Challenge is invited to your Discord server, it assigns secret missions and manages factions automatically. You and your friends complete challenges, earn points, and influence the outcome of the faction wars.

Use commands to check your current mission, faction status, points, and interact with other players through accusations or alliances. The bot keeps the gameplay fun and engaging without requiring constant manual admin intervention.

Popular Commands

!mission - View your current secret mission
!complete - Submit proof and complete your mission
!faction - Check your faction and faction leaderboard
!accuse @user - Accuse another player of betrayal
!points - Show your total secret points
!help - Show detailed help and command list
